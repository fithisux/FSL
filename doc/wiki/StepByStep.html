Step By Step Guides

The step by step guides are provided here as simple recipes to introduce the use of a particular type of analysis. However, FSL is a very flexible toolset and there are many variants for each kind of analysis beyond those presented here. These outlines are therefore not universal recommendations, but intended as a first introduction for learning how to use tools in FSL.

  • Initial Processing (for all modalities)

Structural Imaging

  • Brain Extraction (BET)

  • Tissue-Type Segmentation and Bias-Field Correction (FAST)

  • Subcortical Structure Segmentation (FIRST)

  • Voxel-Based Morphometry (VBM)

Diffusion Imaging

  • Diffusion Tensor Fitting (DTIFIT)

  • TBSS (voxel-wise analysis of local diffusion measures)

FMRI Imaging

  • First-Level Task FMRI (FEAT)

  • Higher-Level Task FMRI (FEAT)

  • Resting-State FMRI (dual regression)
